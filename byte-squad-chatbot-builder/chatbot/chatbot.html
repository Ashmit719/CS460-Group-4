What the knobs do
  • Learning rate: how fast weights move; too high can make loss explode.
  • Warmup + decay: eases into training and slowly lowers LR for stability.
  • Weight decay: gently shrinks weights to reduce overfitting.
  • Label smoothing: softens targets so the model does not lock in too hard.
  • Block size: context length; bigger means more memory and slower steps.
  • Top-K/Top-P/Temp: generation-only knobs, they do not change training.
